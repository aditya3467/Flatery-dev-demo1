A simple, 3-step process

We've simplified property management for you.

  • 1

    Tenant Raises Request

    Tenants can easily submit maintenance or service requests through the intuitive Flatery app or web portal.

  • 2

    Owner Approves

    You receive an instant notification, review the details, and approve the request with a single tap.

  • 3

    Service Provider Resolves

    The assigned service provider is notified, resolves the issue, and marks the request as complete. Everyone stays in the loop.
